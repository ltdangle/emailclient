Question: What is the single most revealing thing about any person?
Answer from Vivian Zhang
Pretend their house is on fire. Then observe what they run or look to first. It could be a material possession. Could be their loved ones.
When Sherlock did this to Irene Adler, she immediately gave away the position of her most prized possession- her phone with all the contacts that allowed her to maintain control and power.
